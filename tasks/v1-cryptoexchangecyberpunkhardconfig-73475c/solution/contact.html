Knowledge Base

Frequently asked

12 questions across 4 categories. Not what you need? Ask the chat.

◈ Account

How long does KYC verification take?
Standard KYC is fully automated and typically completes within 3 minutes. Enhanced due diligence for VIP 3+ tiers or accounts above $250k can take up to 48 hours.
Can I have more than one VaultX account?
Yes — you may operate up to three sub-accounts under one parent. Each sub-account inherits the parent's VIP tier and shares the insurance fund coverage.
How do I reset 2FA if I lose my device?
Use the “Emergency 2FA reset” flow in Security settings. Requires video KYC, matching selfie, and a 72-hour cooling period during which withdrawals are frozen.

⌘ Trading

What is the minimum order size?
Spot: $5 USD equivalent. Perpetuals: 0.001 BTC equivalent. Options: 0.01 contract. VIP 4+ may request reduced minimums.
Do you offer stop-loss and take-profit?
Yes, plus trailing stops, iceberg orders, TWAP, and conditional OCO orders. Native APIs support all order types.
What is the max leverage on perps?
Up to 125× on BTC and ETH perpetuals. Lower for smaller-cap markets. Leverage caps auto-adjust based on position size and open interest.

❄ Security

Are my funds insured?
Yes — a $412M standing insurance fund covers exchange-side losses. All customer assets are held 1:1 with a 104.2% reserve ratio verifiable via Merkle proof.
What happens if VaultX gets hacked?
Insurance fund is drawn on first, then the emergency treasury. In 8 years of operations, VaultX has never lost a single customer sat. Independent audits published quarterly.
Can I use hardware wallets?
Yes. Ledger, Trezor, YubiKey and any FIDO2/WebAuthn-compatible device is supported for 2FA and withdrawal signing.

◎ Withdrawals

Why is my withdrawal pending?
Withdrawals process within 30 minutes on average. Larger amounts (>$100k) may enter a compliance review queue. Track live status under Wallet → Activity.
Can I whitelist withdrawal addresses?
Yes — enable address whitelisting in Security → Withdrawal Rules. New addresses have a mandatory 24-hour cooling period before first use.
Are there daily withdrawal limits?
Default limit is $500k/day for verified users. VIP 3+ can request higher limits (up to $10M/day) with additional verification.
