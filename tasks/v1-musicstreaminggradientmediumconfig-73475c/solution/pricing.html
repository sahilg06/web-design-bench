Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you tap subscribe.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your Account page in two taps — you'll keep Premium access until the end of your current billing period.
What is lossless HiFi audio?
Lossless streams your music at up to 24-bit / 192 kHz — identical to the master file the artist made in the studio. Studio-grade quality on compatible headphones and speakers.
How does the Family plan share work?
Family accounts share a plan manager and up to five additional members. Each person keeps their own listening history, playlists, and recommendations — no crossover.
Do I need a credit card for the free trial?
No. The 30-day trial is completely credit-card free. You'll only be asked for payment if you decide to continue with Premium after the trial ends.
Can I download music for offline listening?
Any Premium tier lets you download up to 10,000 tracks on six devices. Perfect for flights, subway rides, or anywhere Wi-Fi is scarce.
How do I qualify for the student discount?
Verify your enrollment through our partner SheerID with a valid student email or ID. Eligibility must be re-verified once every twelve months.
