The studio

We design brands that move — and feel like they belong to the future.

Founded in 2019 by four designers who wanted to work at the seam between graphic design, animation, and product, Studio Lumina builds identity systems that behave. Every logo has a loop. Every layout has a rhythm. Every touchpoint carries the same pulse — so a brand is recognizable not just by how it looks, but by how it lives.

Meet the studio →
Capabilities

Four disciplines, one moving system.

01 — Brand

Kinetic identity systems

Logo behaviors, motion principles, animated marks, and evolving type systems that stay coherent across every surface.

02 — Product

Interface motion & prototyping

Interaction choreography for web, iOS, and Android — grounded in accessibility and shipped in real production code.

03 — Campaign

Launch films & campaigns

From 6-second social loops to 90-second brand films — art direction, animation, sound, and delivery under one roof.

04 — Spatial

Environmental & immersive

LED architecture, pavilion design, and generative visuals for cultural moments, product launches, and museums.
