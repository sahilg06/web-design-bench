The studio

Eleven designers,
one shared pulse.

Studio Lumina was founded in Berlin in 2019 by four designers who had spent a decade at big agencies and wanted to build something smaller, quieter, and more precise. Six years later we're a team of eleven across Berlin and Lisbon.

What we believe

Five values
we design by.

Written down not because they're clever, but because we return to them when a project gets hard. If we're breaking one, we usually know something is off.

— 01

Motion has meaning

Every transition, ease curve, and loop is a decision — not decoration. We defend every millisecond.

— 02

Small on purpose

We stay under twelve people so the founders touch every project. That's the whole business model.

— 03

Ship, don't showcase

A beautiful mockup that never launches is a failure. We stay engaged until the work is live and measured.

— 04

Restraint is a feature

The best moves are quiet. If we can achieve the same feeling with less, we always cut back.

— 05

Teach as you go

We handoff systems your team can extend, not black boxes only we can maintain. Documentation is a deliverable.
