Studio Story

EST. 2019 // FOUNDED IN A HELSINKI BASEMENT
Original crew: 3 artists, 2 coders, 1 composer, 1 very patient dog.
Mission: build the kind of games we grew up loving — pixel-perfect, hand-crafted, weird in all the right ways.
2019: First release, HEXFALL SAGA, funded on a shoestring.
2021: BYTE KINGDOM breaks 500K units. Basement graduates to actual office.
2024: STELLAR DRIFT wins Best Indie at IGF. Team grows to 24 people.
Today: still handcrafting worlds, one pixel at a time.
