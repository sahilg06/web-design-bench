Our Disciplines

Six core practices anchor our national work — each led by partners recognized among the leading practitioners in their field.

CM

Corporate & M&A

Sterling represents strategic and financial buyers in transactions valued from $50 million to over $10 billion. Our team advises boards on governance, securities compliance, and shareholder relations. We are trusted counsel to nine of the twenty largest asset managers in North America.

Key Clients: Ashford Industries, Meridian Capital, Halcyon Partners
LT

Litigation

We handle bet-the-company disputes, class actions, and complex commercial litigation in federal and state courts nationwide. Our trial team includes former federal prosecutors and appellate specialists. Recent victories include a $2.4B jury verdict in the Southern District of New York.

Key Clients: Continental Insurance, Northgate Bank, Vertex Pharmaceuticals
RE

Real Estate

From landmark urban developments to portfolio-scale industrial acquisitions, our real estate practice combines transactional depth with regulatory insight. We closed more than $8B in real estate transactions in the past fiscal year across twenty-two states.

Key Clients: Harbor Development Group, Sterling REIT, Cedarwood Holdings
EM

Employment

We counsel employers on workforce strategy, executive compensation, restrictive covenants, and internal investigations. Our attorneys defend clients in wage-and-hour class actions and represent them before the EEOC, NLRB, and state agencies.

Key Clients: Regional health systems, Fortune 200 retailers, national logistics firms
IP

Intellectual Property

Our IP team handles patent prosecution, trademark portfolios, trade secret disputes, and technology licensing for life sciences, software, and industrial clients. We have filed more than 1,400 patent applications on behalf of clients in the past five years.

Key Clients: Vertex Pharmaceuticals, Nexus Robotics, Ardent BioSciences
TX

Tax

We advise on federal, state, and international tax planning for corporate transactions, private equity structures, and family enterprises. Our team includes former Treasury Department attorneys and Big Four transfer pricing specialists.

Key Clients: Meridian Capital, Halcyon Family Office, cross-border industrial groups

Notable Cases

A representative selection of recent matters — details published with client permission.

M&A · 2024

Ashford — Halcyon Merger

Represented Ashford Industries in its $6.8B all-stock combination with Halcyon Group, creating the largest specialty chemicals manufacturer in North America. Managed antitrust clearance in three jurisdictions and coordinated a synchronized closing across seven regulatory bodies.

Litigation · 2023

Continental Insurance Verdict

Secured a $2.4B jury verdict for Continental Insurance in a landmark reinsurance coverage dispute in the Southern District of New York. The verdict was affirmed on appeal and cited by the Second Circuit as a leading authority on bad-faith standards.

Real Estate · 2023

Harbor District Redevelopment

Structured the acquisition, entitlement, and financing of the 42-acre Harbor District mixed-use development in Boston — a $1.9B project that produced 2,100 units of housing and 400,000 square feet of commercial space.
