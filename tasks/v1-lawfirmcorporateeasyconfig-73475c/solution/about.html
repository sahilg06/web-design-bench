Our History

Founded in 1982 on a Simple Premise

Sterling & Associates was established in the spring of 1982 by Jonathan R. Sterling and three colleagues who had grown convinced that clients deserved a different kind of law firm — one grounded in enduring relationships rather than transactional volume, in judgment rather than billable-hour metrics.

From a single office on Madison Avenue with a handful of attorneys, the firm has grown deliberately to more than two hundred lawyers across seven offices in the United States and Europe. That growth has never come at the expense of the firm's founding character: partners still lead every engagement, and matters are staffed for excellence, not leverage.

Today, our clients include Fortune 500 corporations, sovereign wealth funds, family enterprises, and every major American financial institution. Many have retained the firm for decades.

Our Values

Four principles have guided every engagement since the day the firm was founded.

I.

Integrity

We do not compromise on candor. Clients hear our honest assessment of the law and the facts — even when that assessment is unwelcome. Trust, once established, is our most valuable asset.

II.

Excellence

Every document, every argument, and every strategic decision reflects the standards our founding partners set forty years ago. Good is not sufficient when our clients' most significant matters are at stake.

III.

Client Focus

Our clients' interests come first. We invest the time to understand their business, their people, and their long-term objectives — because the best counsel arises from genuine knowledge of the client.

IV.

Teamwork

Complex matters demand collaboration across practices, offices, and disciplines. We assemble the right team for every engagement and share credit generously across the firm.

Community

Investing in the Communities We Serve

Sterling attorneys contribute more than 15,000 hours annually to community service, board leadership, and civic engagement. Our firm underwrites scholarship programs at four public universities and provides board-level support to twenty-three non-profit organizations across the cities in which our attorneys live and work.

Our founding partners believed that a great law firm must be a great neighbor. That conviction remains at the heart of the firm.

Pro Bono Commitment

In 2024, our attorneys devoted more than 42,000 hours to pro bono representation — among the highest per-attorney contributions in the AmLaw 200.

Diversity & Inclusion Statement

Sterling & Associates believes that a diverse and inclusive firm is a stronger firm. We are Mansfield Certified Plus, and our leadership reflects our commitment: 48% of our partnership is comprised of women, and 35% identify as attorneys of color. We recruit intentionally from a broad range of law schools and backgrounds, and we invest sustained resources in the development, mentorship, and advancement of every attorney at the firm. We believe our clients are best served when they are counseled by teams whose experiences and perspectives are as varied as the challenges they face.
