Frequently asked questions

If we haven't answered it below, our team is a click away — reach out any time.

Do you have a free tier for hobbyists and prototypes?+

Yes. Every new account gets 100,000 free inferences per month, forever. No credit card required, no cliff, no forced upgrade if you stay within the tier.

Can I run NexaAI inside my own cloud account?+

Enterprise customers can deploy the NexaAI control and data planes into their AWS, GCP, or Azure environments. Data, model weights, and telemetry never leave your VPC.

Which foundation models are included out of the box?+

Over 400 open models spanning text, vision, and multimodal — including Llama, Mistral, Qwen, Whisper, CLIP, and SDXL — plus a curated set of NexaAI-tuned models optimized for latency and cost.

How does billing work when I exceed my included inferences?+

Overage is billed at the on-demand rate for your model class at the end of the month. Set spend caps on individual endpoints and receive alerts at 50%, 75%, and 90% of your budget.

Do you offer discounts for startups or academic research?+

Yes. Our Ignite program provides $50,000 in credits over 12 months for seed and Series A startups, and academic labs receive 50% off Scale plan pricing.

What compliance certifications do you hold?+

SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, HIPAA (Enterprise plan), GDPR, and FedRAMP Moderate. Attestation letters are available under NDA before purchase.

How do you keep my training data private?+

Training data is encrypted at rest with your CMK, never used to improve shared models, and can be sequestered inside a private tenancy pool available on Scale and Enterprise plans.

Can I bring my own model weights?+

Absolutely. Upload PyTorch, JAX, ONNX, or SafeTensors artifacts through the CLI or push directly from a training job. NexaAI handles compilation, packaging, and rollout.

What happens if I need to leave the platform?+

Your models, features, and audit logs are yours. Export everything at any time via the CLI in standard open formats. No lock-in, no exit fees.

Is there a way to test with a live human first?+

Yes — every prospect gets a 30-minute architecture review with a solutions engineer, whether or not you buy. Book directly from the contact page.

Need something custom?

Regulated data, custom regions, hybrid deployments, and multi-cloud architectures are our specialty. Let's design your rollout together.
