Studio — Zurich, Copenhagen, Lisbon

The Practice

Forma Studio is a partnership of thirty-two architects, engineers, and researchers, founded in Zurich in 2008 by Elin Voss and Kristian Bru.

About

A partnership, deliberately small.

We have chosen, at every juncture in our growth, to stay smaller than the market rewards. A studio of thirty-two allows the founding partners to remain in the drawings for every commission, and every drawing to remain in the hands of someone who has visited the site.

Our first office opened above a bookshop in Zurich's Kreis 4 in 2008. Copenhagen followed in 2016; Lisbon in 2022. The three studios operate as one, on shared calendars and shared drawings; senior architects rotate quarterly between them.

We do not pitch. Our commissions arrive by recommendation or by open competition, both of which reward the work rather than the sales instrument. We turn down more work than we accept and consider that a discipline, not a boast.

Chronology

2008 — Present
2008
Forma Studio founded by Elin Voss and Kristian Bru above the Buchhandlung Neumann in Zurich Kreis 4. First commission: a two-room extension for a private client in Uster.
2012
First cultural commission — Fribourg Municipal Archive — completed. Selected for Swiss Architecture Yearbook.
2016
Copenhagen studio opens under partner Yusuke Ando. Team crosses twelve architects. Publication of monograph First Decade.
2020
Rhine Study Centre completed. Adoption of the Six Principles of Sustainability as studio-wide practice policy.
2022
Lisbon studio opens under partner Marta Reis. Nordhavn Masterplan handed over to the City of Copenhagen.
2025
Kelvin House completed. Elin Voss shortlisted for the Pritzker. Studio commits to whole-life carbon reporting on every future commission.

Work with us

Open positions are advertised twice a year, in March and September. We prize slow readers, careful drawers, and people who can hold their opinions lightly. Speculative applications are welcomed but answered only in those months.
