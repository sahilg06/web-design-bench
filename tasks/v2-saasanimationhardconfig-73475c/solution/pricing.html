FAQ

Answers to common questions

What counts as a workflow run?

A run is any single end-to-end execution of a workflow, whether it succeeds, fails, or is intentionally halted. Multi-branch runs count once, and internal retries are always free.

Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?

No. The 14-day Pro trial requires only an email address. If you don't upgrade at the end, your account automatically downgrades to the free Starter tier — nothing is charged.

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from your billing dashboard. Upgrades apply instantly and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Absolutely — pay annually and save 20% on Pro. Enterprise contracts are multi-year and come with committed-use discounts.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or startups?

Yes. Registered non-profits receive 50% off the Pro plan. Startups accepted into a partner accelerator get one full year of Pro free — email startups@flowsync.io.

How does Enterprise pricing work?

Enterprise contracts are annual and scoped to your run volume, region, and compliance requirements. Contact sales for a tailored quote — typical deals close in under two weeks.

What happens if I exceed my plan's run quota?

Workflows continue to run without interruption. Overages are billed at $0.001 per additional run on Starter, $0.0006 on Pro. We'll notify you at 80% and 100% usage so there are never surprises.

Not sure which plan fits?

Our team will map your existing tooling to a FlowSync plan in one 20-minute call — with a locked quote and migration checklist.
