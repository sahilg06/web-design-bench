Our Atelier

Rue Saint-Honoré, 1987

Founded above a bookseller in the eighth arrondissement, Maison Élégance began as the private studio of Camille Lévesque — a former première d'atelier at a storied Parisian couture house.

Nearly four decades later, the same hands still finish every hem, every buttonhole, every silk-lined seam. The Maison remains independent, family-run, and quietly devoted to the craft.
