Frequently asked questions

Answers to the most common questions we hear from BiteBuzz customers.

🛒 Orders & Delivery

How long does delivery usually take? +
Most orders arrive in 20–35 minutes, depending on your distance from the restaurant and current demand. You'll see a live ETA before checkout and real-time updates once your rider is on the way.
Can I schedule an order for later? +
Yes! Choose "Schedule for later" at checkout and pick any 15-minute time slot up to 7 days in advance. Perfect for planning parties, meetings, or Sunday breakfast.
What if a restaurant is out of an item? +
The restaurant will call or message you through the app. You can pick a substitute, remove the item for a refund, or cancel the order at no charge.

💳 Payments & Refunds

What payment methods do you accept? +
Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, BiteBuzz gift cards, and BiteBuzz Points. Cash on delivery is available in select cities.
How long do refunds take? +
Refunds are typically processed within 24 hours. Depending on your bank, funds may take 3–5 business days to appear on your statement. Credit to your BiteBuzz wallet is instant.
Why was I charged twice? +
You likely see an authorization hold plus the final charge. The hold drops off within 3–7 business days automatically. If both charges settle, contact us and we'll refund immediately.

👤 Account & Loyalty

How do BiteBuzz Points work? +
Earn 1 point per $1 spent (2× on featured deals). Redeem for free delivery, discounts, or exclusive items. Points never expire as long as you order at least once every 12 months.
How do I delete my account? +
Head to Settings → Privacy → Delete Account, or email privacy@bitebuzz.com. We'll process your request within 30 days and permanently erase your data as required by law.
