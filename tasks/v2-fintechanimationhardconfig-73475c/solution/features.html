Platform capabilities

Every discipline of wealth, in one system.

Vault replaces the tangle of custodial portals, portfolio accounting software, and manual spreadsheets that most offices operate on today. One ledger. One interface. Every asset class.

Unified Ledger

Append-only, double-entry accounting across cash, listed securities, private funds, real estate, and digital assets. Reconciled to custodian truth every four hours.

Portfolio Analytics

Time-weighted and money-weighted return calculations, factor exposure, drawdown analytics, and scenario stress testing native to the ledger.

Tax Optimization

Automated tax-lot selection with wash-sale monitoring, harvesting suggestions, and 1099-B / Schedule D packages exported year-round.

Custodian Network

Direct connectivity to 40+ prime brokers and custodians including Fidelity, Schwab, JPM, BNY Mellon, Northern Trust, Anchorage, and Coinbase Prime.

Compliance Suite

Rule-based pre-trade compliance checks, exposure limits, insider blackout windows, and audit trails ready for SEC, FINRA, and MAS review.

Reporting Engine

Board-grade PDF packs, LP-quality quarterly statements, and CSV / JSON exports piped to your BI stack. White-labelled and delivered on schedule.
