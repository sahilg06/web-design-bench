Security & trust

Custody-grade posture. Client-side control.

Vault is built to the standard institutional custodians hold themselves to. Your data is encrypted before it is stored, signed as it moves, and audited on every read.

AES

Encryption at rest & in transit

AES-256-GCM at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit. Per-tenant key encryption keys managed in a FIPS 140-2 Level 3 HSM. You may bring your own KMS key on Enterprise.

MFA

Zero-trust access controls

Hardware-backed MFA (WebAuthn / FIDO2) is mandatory for every session. Fine-grained RBAC with quorum approvals for any read of raw beneficiary data.

LOG

Immutable audit trail

Every read and write is recorded to a tamper-evident, hash-chained log with 7-year WORM retention. Auditors get read-only access on their own credentials — never yours.

VPC

Isolated infrastructure

Multi-region deployment across AWS us-east-1, eu-west-1, and ap-southeast-1. Enterprise plans run in customer-owned VPCs with peering only to your allow-listed partners.
