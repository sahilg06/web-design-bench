About Wanderlust

We're a small crew of certified travel designers, ex-hoteliers and ex-guides. Since 2010 we've been quietly building trips that our own families actually want to take.

👋
Founder's story

It started with a broken itinerary in Bhutan.

In 2010 our founder Amara Devi — then a hotel manager in Paro — watched an exhausted family unravel because their previous agency had double-booked their lodge and their guide didn't speak English.

She fixed it in a night. They cried on their last day. On the flight home she started sketching a different kind of travel company: one where a real person answers the phone at 3am, where guides are paid twice the industry rate, and where the itinerary is written for you, not sold to you.

Fifteen years later, Wanderlust has grown to 34 people across three offices, but the promise hasn't budged. Every trip is still signed off by a human. Amara still leads two expedition trips a year — usually the ones no one else will run.

🌱 Our commitment

Travel that gives more than it takes.

We've been fully carbon-neutral since 2019 — not through cheap offsets, but through direct partnerships with reforestation projects in Bali, Peru and Kenya.

Every trip funds one full day of a local guide's living wage. Every group of ten offsets the flights of one solo traveler. And every itinerary is audited by a third party for its environmental footprint before it goes to sale.

  • 🌳 Planted 84,000+ native trees across three continents
  • 💧 Every trip funds 30 days of clean water for a rural household
  • 🐘 Only partner with ethical wildlife sanctuaries — no captive rides, no petting
  • 💼 Guides earn 2× regional industry median
